Create a clear written notice that declines searches, questioning, conversation, and informal contact while directing communication to a designated legal channel. Everything runs locally in this file.

Card details

The left QR code opens this reference. The default is the official U.N. declaration page.

This is a personal boundary notice, not a magic legal shield or a guarantee that every sentence has legal force. It does not authorize anyone to ignore a lawful order, valid service of process, or a genuine emergency. Its purpose is to clearly withhold voluntary consent and identify the channel through which non-emergency communication will be accepted.
