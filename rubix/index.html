Rubik's Cube Solving Instructions

Step 1: Get The White Cross On Top

Move the white cross edge piece to the top layer:

F U' R U

Step 2: Complete White Side While Creating The T's

Bring the white corner piece to its correct position and orientation:

R' D' R D

Step 3: Flip For Top As Yellow Now & Finish Middle Layer

To Left:

U' L' U L U F U' F'

To Right:

U R U' R' U' F' U F

Step 4: Get Yellow Cross (Location Doesn't Matter)

Arrange the yellow cross pieces roughly:

F R U R' U' F'

Step 5: Yellow Cross (Fix Locations)

Adjust the positions of the yellow cross pieces:

R U R' U R U2 R' U

Step 6: Corners Into Correct Spots (They can be spun wrong)

Move the yellow corner pieces to their correct positions:

U R U' L' U R' U' L

Step 7: Finish Spinning The Corners Right Way Round Now

Adjust the orientation of the yellow corner pieces:

R' D' R D
