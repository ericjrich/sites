Tecno Wasino JJ-1 Turret Zero Point Setting Procedure - FANUC 18i-TB

Procedure Overview

This procedure will guide you through resetting the zero point on the Tecno Wasino JJ-1â€™s servo-controlled turret using the FANUC 18i-TB controller. Follow each step precisely to avoid errors and ensure correct turret operation.

Steps

  1. Turn Off Power At Control And Main For 30sec. Then Turn All back on.
  2. 1st Boot:
    MDI Mode
    Select [L] or [R] Lathe (Which Side Are We Calibrating)
    Press [OFFSET/SETTING] Until We See Parameter Write (Set It To 1)
    Press [RESET]+[CAN]
    Press [SYSTEM]
    Press [PMC MAINT]
    Press [COUNTERS]
    Find the parameter by is 'C' number (Set According To Side):
    LH: C40
    RH: C80
    Preset= 8
    Tool #= 0
    Press [KeepRelays]
    Find 'K16' (Set According To Side)
    LH: K16.0= 1
    RH: K16.1= 1
    Press [REF] (enables unclamp ability)
    Press [+Z] (unclamps turret)
    Press [+X] or [-X] to rotate turret more that 1 full spin
    Turn Down The Feed Rate to lowest increment above 0
    Continue using [+X] & [-X] to align T1 into position
    Clamp the Turret Head Using [-Z]
    Verify Clamping:
    Check that the turret head is securely clamped.
    If the turret does not clamp correctly, unclamp and keep trying until we get T1 properly re-clamped
  3. Power Cycle the CNC @ controller (off for at least 3 sec):
  4. 2nd Boot:
    Select [L] or [R] Lathe (Which Side Are We Calibrating)
    Press [OFFSET/SETTING] Until We See Parameter Write (Set It To 1)
    Press [RESET]+[CAN]
    Press [SYSTEM]
    Press [PMC MAINT]
    Press [KeepRelays]
    Find 'K16' (Set According To Side)
    LH: K16.0= 1
    RH: K16.1= 1
    Press [REF] (enables unclamp ability)
    Press [+Z] (unclamps turret)
    Press [-Z] (clamps turret)
    Now hold down for 3 sec [-Z]+[REF]+[LH or RH]
  5. Final Power Cycle:
    Do a full shutdown at control and main PWR
    Wait 30sec Then Turn On Main Only
    Then Hold in [RESET]+[CAN] while booting up controller (hold until fully booted)
    Home All Axis's
    Put Machine In [RH] or [LH] (whichever you need)
  6. Test Turret Indexing:
    Test manual indexing by pressing the TurretIndex Button For your side.
    Now Test automatic indexing, use a T-code command in MDI mode to confirm correct movement.
    Example MDI Command= T0808
    Test both clockwise and counterclockwise indexing to ensure smooth movement and secure clamping at each position.
    Example MDI Command= T0202

    When operating correctly, PMC COUNTERs c40/c80 "Current" should change as you index manually.
